My Starbucks Go-Tos

I don't go to Starbucks often as I usually like to get my coffee from smaller local cafes (also Starbucks has raised their prices while the quality of their drinks stayed the same). Yet sometimes when I just want a quick drink to pick up and go, I'll go to my closest Starbucks and get one of my usual three drinks. Depending on the weather, I'll rotate between getting a Pink Drink, a Caramel Mocha, and an Iced Matcha Latte.
