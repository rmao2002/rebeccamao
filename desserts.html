My Favorite Desserts

Although I don't have a crazy sweet tooth, somethings about the beautiful designs of desserts draw me in. As you scroll down you'll see my favorite deserts broken down into 3 categories: summer time desserts, fall time desserts and desserts that have childhool memories attached to them.

Summer Time Desserts

Lemon pound cake, mochi ice cream, and tiramisu are all desserts I enjoy indulging in during the hot summer months. They're all light in texture or fruit flavored which gives a refreshing taste, perfect for the summer!

Fall Time Desserts

Once the weather gets colder, I love baking and eating more heavier desserts utilizing seasonal ingredients. My favorite desserts to cuddle up with during the colder months are apple crumble, banana bread, and baked egg tarts.

Desserts That Envoke Nostalgia

The desserts shown below are all desserts that envoke childhood nostalgia. As a child, one of the few things my mom knew how to bake was a strawberry cream roll cake. I remember getting so excited when I saw her rolling up the cake with fresh whipped cream. Another dessert that I rarely get to indulge in today are Chinese bakery style cakes. These cakes differ from others as they are made of light yellow sponge cake with a variety of seasonal fruit in between the layers covered in a thick layer of whipped cream. These were the cakes my family would get to celebrate birthdays. Lastly, my childhood isn't complete without chocolate chip cookies. These were the first thing I ever baked myself. It's also a classic, who doesn't like chocolate chip cookies?
